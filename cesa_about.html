CESA Sports Committee

(Civil Engineering Students’ Association)

The Department of Civil Engineering, Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology (SGGSIE&T), Nanded, proudly presents its annual departmental fest — CESA CUP 2025 and CENTFEST 2025, organized under the banner of the Civil Engineering Students’ Association (CESA).

This grand event aims to foster a healthy balance between technical excellence and sportsmanship among students while nurturing leadership, creativity, and team spirit within the Civil Engineering fraternity.

CESA CUP

The sports segment of the fest, CESA CUP, brings together a series of energetic and competitive sporting events including Open Cricket, Turf Cricket, Basketball, Rink Football, Tug of War, Kho-Kho, Volleyball, Badminton, Chess, Carrom, and Athletics. These events encourage teamwork, endurance, and discipline — core values that every engineer must embody.

CENFEST

The technical counterpart, CENTFEST, focuses on innovation, design, and practical problem-solving through a variety of technical competitions such as CADWAR, STAADWAR, Treasure Hunt, Revit Clash, Build Blind, Town Planning, and Tender-X. This platform empowers participants to apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges, promoting professional growth and collaboration.

Together, CESA CUP and CENTFEST create a holistic experience that celebrates both the intellectual and physical spirit of engineering students, making it one of the most awaited events of the academic year.The Sports Committee of CESA College is dedicated to organizing a wide range of sporting events, tournaments, and fitness initiatives throughout the year. Our mission is to foster a vibrant sports culture, encourage students to stay active, and showcase their athletic talents.
